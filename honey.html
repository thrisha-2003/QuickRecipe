CRISPY HONEY CHICKEN

Incredients
    • 1 pound (about 450g) boneless, skinless chicken thighs or breasts, cut into bite-sized pieces
    • 1 cup cornstarch
    • 1 teaspoon baking powder
    • 1/2 teaspoon salt
    • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
    • 2 eggs, beaten
    • Oil for frying
    • For the Sauce:
    • 1/4 cup honey
    • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
    • 1 tablespoon ketchup
    • 1 tablespoon rice vinegar
    • 1 teaspoon sesame oil
    • 2 cloves garlic, minced
    • 1 teaspoon fresh ginger, grated
    • Sesame seeds and green onions for garnish

    Directions
    1. Step 1: Prepare the Chicken
      • In a bowl, mix cornstarch, baking powder, salt, and black pepper.
      • Dredge each piece of chicken in the cornstarch mixture, shaking off excess.
      • Dip the coated chicken in beaten eggs, ensuring they are well-covered.
    2. Step 2: Fry the Chicken
      • Heat oil in a pan or deep fryer to 350°F (175°C).
      • Fry the coated chicken pieces in batches until golden brown and crispy. Drain on paper towels.
    3. Step 3: Make the Sauce
      • In a separate saucepan, mix honey, soy sauce, ketchup, rice vinegar, sesame oil, minced garlic, and grated ginger.
      • Bring the sauce to a simmer and cook for a few minutes until it thickens slightly.
    4. Step 4: Coat the Chicken in Sauce
      • Toss the fried chicken in the honey sauce until evenly coated.
    5. Step 5: Garnish and Serve
      • Transfer the crispy honey chicken to a serving platter.
      • Garnish with sesame seeds and chopped green onions.
      • Serve hot and enjoy!
