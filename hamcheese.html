HAMCHEESE BISCUIT

Incredients
  • 1 can pre-made biscuits
  • 8 eggs
  • 8 slices deli ham
  • 2 cups shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1 scallion, sliced thin for garnish (optional)

Directions
  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees Fahrenheit.
  2. Heavily grease a standard, 12-cup muffin tin (the top, too).
  3. Divide the eight biscuits into the cups and press them down and up into the sides of the cup. Flatten to make sure there is a big well.
  4. Rip the ham slice into a few pieces and lay them into the well. It's good if it hangs out as that will provide some yummy, crispy edges.
  5. Carefully crack an egg into each well.
  6. Bake for 20 minutes.
  7. At the 20-minute mark, sprinkle the cheese on the tops of the eggs and bake for a few more minutes.
  8. Carefully remove from the tin and serve with sliced scallions.
