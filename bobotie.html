BOBOTIE

Incredients
  • 1 kg ground beef or lamb
  • 2 slices of white bread
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons curry powder
  • 1 teaspoon ground turmeric
  • 1 teaspoon ground coriander
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1/2 cup chutney
  • 1/4 cup apricot jam
  • 3 tablespoons vinegar
  • 1/2 cup raisins
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 3 large eggs
  • Bay leaves for garnish
  • Oil for frying

Directions
  1. Step 1: Preheat the Oven
    • Preheat your oven to 180°C (350°F).
  2. Step 2: Soak the Bread
    • In a bowl, soak the bread slices in the milk until softened.
    • Mash the soaked bread into a paste.
  3. Step 3: Prepare the Meat Mixture
    • In a pan, heat oil and sauté the chopped onion and garlic until soft.
    • Add ground meat and brown it.
    • Stir in curry powder, turmeric, coriander, and cumin.
    • Add the bread paste, chutney, apricot jam, vinegar, raisins, salt, and pepper.
    • Cook the mixture until well combined and cooked through.
  4. Step 4: Assemble the Bobotie
    • Transfer the meat mixture to a greased baking dish.
    • Smooth the top with a spatula.
  5. Step 5: Prepare the Egg Topping
    • In a bowl, beat the eggs and pour them over the meat mixture.
    • Place bay leaves on top for garnish.
  6. Step 6: Bake
    • Bake in the preheated oven for about 35-40 minutes or until the top is set and golden.
  7. Step 7: Serve
    • Allow the Bobotie to cool slightly before serving.
    • Serve with rice, chutney, and a side salad.
