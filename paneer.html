PANEER MASALA

Incredients
    • 250g paneer (cottage cheese), cubed
    • 2 onions, finely chopped
    • 2 tomatoes, pureed
    • 1/2 cup yogurt
    • 1/2 cup cashew nuts, soaked in water for 2 hours
    • 2 green chilies, chopped
    • 1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste
    • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
    • 1 teaspoon coriander powder
    • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
    • 1/2 teaspoon red chili powder
    • 1/2 teaspoon garam masala
    • 1/2 teaspoon kasuri methi (dried fenugreek leaves)
    • 2 tablespoons cooking oil
    • Salt to taste
    • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish

    Directions

    1. Step 1:

      Heat oil in a pan, add cumin seeds, and let them splutter.

    2. Step 2:

      Add chopped onions and sauté until golden brown.

    3. Step 3:

      Add ginger-garlic paste and green chilies. Sauté for 2 minutes.

    4. Step 4:

      Add pureed tomatoes and cook until the oil separates.

    5. Step 5:

      In a blender, blend soaked cashew nuts and yogurt to make a smooth paste.

    6. Step 6:

      Add the cashew-yogurt paste to the pan and cook for 3-4 minutes.

    7. Step 7:

      Add coriander powder, turmeric powder, red chili powder, and salt. Mix well.

    8. Step 8:

      Add paneer cubes and cook for another 5-7 minutes, allowing the paneer to absorb the flavors.

    9. Step 9:

      Sprinkle garam masala and kasuri methi. Mix gently.

    10. Step 10:

      Garnish with fresh coriander leaves.

    11. Step 11:

      Serve hot with naan or rice.
