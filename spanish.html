SPANISH OMELETTE

Incredients
  • 4 large eggs
  • 4 medium-sized potatoes, peeled and thinly sliced
  • 1 large onion, thinly sliced
  • 1/2 cup olive oil
  • Salt to taste
  • Optional: Chopped fresh parsley for garnish

Directions
  1. Step 1: Prepare Potatoes and Onions
    • Peel and thinly slice the potatoes and onions.
  2. Step 2: Heat Olive Oil
    • In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat.
  3. Step 3: Cook Potatoes and Onions
    • Add the sliced potatoes and onions to the heated oil.
    • Cook, stirring occasionally, until the potatoes are tender but not browned. This may take about 10-15 minutes.
    • Remove potatoes and onions from the skillet, draining excess oil, and let them cool slightly.
  4. Step 4: Beat Eggs
    • In a large bowl, beat the eggs and season with salt.
  5. Step 5: Combine and Mix
    • Add the cooked potatoes and onions to the beaten eggs.
    • Gently mix until well combined.
  6. Step 6: Cook the Omelette
    • Heat a bit of the drained oil in the skillet over medium heat.
    • Pour the egg mixture into the skillet, spreading it evenly.
    • Cook for a few minutes until the edges set.
  7. Step 7: Flip and Cook
    • Place a plate over the skillet and carefully flip the omelette onto the plate.
    • Slide the omelette back into the skillet, uncooked side down.
    • Cook until the eggs are fully set and the omelette is golden brown.
  8. Step 8: Garnish and Serve
    • Optional: Garnish with chopped fresh parsley.
    • Serve the Spanish omelette warm, sliced into wedges.
