VADA

Incredients
  • 1 cup urad dal (black gram)
  • 1-2 green chilies, finely chopped
  • 1 inch piece of ginger, grated
  • A few curry leaves, finely chopped
  • 1/2 cup finely chopped onions (optional)
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil for deep frying

Directions
  1. Step 1: Soak Urad Dal

    Wash and soak urad dal in water for 3-4 hours.

  2. Step 2: Grind Urad Dal

    Drain the water from the soaked dal and grind it into a smooth paste. Use minimal water while grinding. The batter should be thick and fluffy.

  3. Step 3: Add Flavors

    Add finely chopped green chilies, grated ginger, curry leaves, chopped onions (if using), and salt to the batter. Mix well.

  4. Step 4: Shape Vadas

    1. Heat oil in a deep fryer or a heavy-bottomed pan over medium heat.
    2. Wet your hands with water, take a small portion of the batter, and shape it into a round disc with a hole in the center. You can use a wet cloth or banana leaf to shape the vadas.

  5. Step 5: Fry Vadas

    Carefully slide the shaped vadas into the hot oil. Fry until they turn golden brown, turning them to ensure even cooking.

  6. Step 6: Drain Excess Oil

    Once the vadas are golden brown, use a slotted spoon to remove them from the oil and place them on a paper towel to drain excess oil.

  7. Step 7: Serve

    Serve the vadas hot with coconut chutney, mint chutney, or sambar.
