EGG BHURJI

Incredients
  • 3 eggs
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 1 large tomato, finely chopped
  • 1-2 green chilies, finely chopped
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon cumin powder
  • 1 teaspoon coriander powder
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 tablespoon oil or butter
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish

Directions
  1. Step 1: Prepare Ingredients
    • Finely chop onions, tomatoes, and green chilies.
    • Beat the eggs in a bowl and set aside.
  2. Step 2: Sauté Onions
    • Heat oil or butter in a pan over medium heat.
    • Add chopped onions and sauté until they become translucent.
  3. Step 3: Add Green Chilies and Tomatoes
    • Add chopped green chilies to the onions and sauté for a minute.
    • Add chopped tomatoes and cook until they become soft.
  4. Step 4: Season with Spices
    • Add turmeric powder, red chili powder, cumin powder, coriander powder, and salt. Mix well.
  5. Step 5: Scramble the Eggs
    • Pour the beaten eggs into the pan with the sautéed vegetables.
    • Stir continuously until the eggs are cooked and well combined with the vegetables.
  6. Step 6: Garnish and Serve
    • Garnish with fresh coriander leaves.
    • Serve hot with bread, roti, or as a filling in wraps.
