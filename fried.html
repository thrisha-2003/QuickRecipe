CHICKEN FRIED RICE

Incredients
  • 2 cups cooked jasmine rice (preferably cold, leftover rice)
  • 1 cup cooked and shredded chicken breast
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables (carrots, peas, corn)
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 3 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon oyster sauce
  • 1 teaspoon sesame oil
  • 1/2 cup chopped green onions
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil for cooking
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Directions
  1. Step 1: Prep Ingredients
    • Ensure all ingredients are chopped, shredded, and ready to use.
  2. Step 2: Scramble Eggs
    • In a wok or large skillet, heat vegetable oil over medium-high heat.
    • Pour beaten eggs into the pan and scramble until cooked. Set aside.
  3. Step 3: Sauté Chicken
    • Using the same pan, add a bit more oil if needed.
    • Add minced garlic and sauté until fragrant.
    • Add shredded chicken and cook until it's no longer pink.
  4. Step 4: Add Vegetables
    • Stir in mixed vegetables and cook until they are tender yet slightly crisp.
  5. Step 5: Combine with Rice
    • Add cold, cooked jasmine rice to the pan. Break up any clumps and mix well with the other ingredients.
  6. Step 6: Season
    • Pour soy sauce, oyster sauce, and sesame oil over the rice mixture. Stir to combine.
    • Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  7. Step 7: Add Scrambled Eggs
    • Add the scrambled eggs back into the pan and mix thoroughly.
  8. Step 8: Garnish and Serve
    • Sprinkle chopped green onions over the fried rice and give it a final toss.
    • Serve hot and enjoy your homemade Chicken Fried Rice!
